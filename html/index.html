About this layout

I am sure y'all are intrigued by the title of this website. Simply said, I created this layout to depict the alien and majestic qualities of bugs. Some people will see bugs in their homes and immediately dismiss them as disgusting and distinguishable. Other people will see bugs as a beautiful and diverse necessity to Nature. Some bugs such as dragonflies will eat disease- infested mosquitoes. Other bugs such as mosquitoes will contribute to the spread of malaria. All in all, bugs create a polarized uproar amongst humans, and let's not forget the developer's favorite bug: the "programming bug."
